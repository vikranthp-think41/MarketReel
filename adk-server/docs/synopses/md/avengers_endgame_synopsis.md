The film opens in the aftermath of Thanos's catastrophic snap, which erased half of all life across the universe. The surviving Avengers — Tony Stark, Steve Rogers, Natasha Romanoff, Thor, Bruce Banner, and Clint Barton — are left fractured, grief-stricken, and seemingly without hope.Five years pass. The world has fundamentally changed. Scott Lang escapes from the quantum realm with a radical idea: time travel. Reconvening the team, the Avengers devise a desperate plan — a "time heist" to collect the six Infinity Stones from various points in the past before Thanos can use them. Each mission carries enormous personal weight as heroes revisit pivotal moments in their own histories.The plan succeeds but at great cost — Natasha Romanoff sacrifices herself on Vormir so Clint can retrieve the Soul Stone. Bruce Banner, merging his intellect with the Hulk's strength, successfully uses a makeshift gauntlet to reverse the snap, restoring all lost life. But a past version of Thanos, having learned of their plan, arrives in the present with his full army.What follows is an epic final battle at the ruins of Avengers HQ, where every restored hero returns through sorcerer-opened portals to face Thanos's forces. The climax centers on Tony Stark, who ultimately uses the Infinity Stones himself to erase Thanos and his army — sacrificing his own life in the process.
