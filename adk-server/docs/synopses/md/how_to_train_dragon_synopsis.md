Hiccup, the awkward teenage son of chieftain Stoick the Vast, is a misfit in a culture that prizes brawn over brains. Desperate to prove himself, Hiccup secretly launches a contraption that downs a Night Fury — the most feared and rarely seen dragon — during a raid.Finding the grounded dragon hidden in the forest, Hiccup cannot bring himself to deliver the killing blow, instead forming a tentative connection with the creature he names Toothless. He discovers that Toothless's tail has been injured, preventing flight. Over many secret visits, Hiccup builds Toothless a prosthetic tail fin and learns to ride him — developing the first genuine bond between human and dragon in Berk's history.Meanwhile, Hiccup is enrolled in dragon-fighting school, where his newly acquired knowledge of dragon behavior allows him to seemingly subdue each dragon he faces — impressing his peers and instructors, including his crush Astrid. When Astrid discovers his secret, she initially threatens to expose him but is won over after experiencing flight with Toothless.The central conflict erupts when Stoick captures a dragon who leads him unwittingly to the dragons' nest — ruled by a monstrous queen dragon who forces all other dragons to raid for food. Hiccup and his friends ride their trained dragons to defend Toothless, who defeats the queen. The victory transforms Berk's relationship with dragons forever, and Stoick finally accepts his son.
