MarketReel — Global Film Distribution Intelligence RussiaFilm Censorship & Certification GuidelinesMinistry of Culture of the Russian Federation — Regulatory Intelligence1. Regulatory AuthorityThe Ministry of Culture of the Russian Federation (Министерство культуры) issues distribution licences for all films shown in Russia. All films must obtain a distribution certificate (прокатное удостоверение) before theatrical exhibition. Since 2022, the regulatory environment has significantly tightened. Following Russia's invasion of Ukraine, all major Western studios suspended releases in Russia, making the theatrical market largely non-viable for mainstream Western titles as of 2024.CRITICAL GEOPOLITICAL NOTEFollowing Russia's February 2022 invasion of Ukraine, Disney, Warner Bros., Universal, Sony, and Paramount suspended Russian operations. The market is dominated by domestic productions. Agent must assess current geopolitical context and studio policy before recommending any Russia theatrical release.2.
