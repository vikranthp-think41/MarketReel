Regulatory AuthorityGermany's film classification is administered by the Freiwillige Selbstkontrolle der Filmwirtschaft (FSK), a voluntary self-regulatory body operating under the Youth Protection Act (Jugendschutzgesetz, JuSchG). FSK classifications are legally binding for theatrical exhibition and physical/digital media. For content related to National Socialism or Holocaust denial, additional oversight applies under Strafgesetzbuch §130 (Volksverhetzung/incitement) and §86a (unconstitutional symbols).2. These are LEGALLY PROHIBITED — not merely high-risk classification outcomes:Holocaust denial — depicting, affirming, or minimising the Holocaust is a criminal offence under §130 StGB (Volksverhetzung). Automatic referral to prosecutors.Nazi symbols in a glorifying or approving context — swastikas, SS insignia, Hitler salutes are prohibited under §86a StGB. EXCEPTION: use in clearly anti-Nazi, artistic, or educational context is fully legal (e.g. Schindler's List, Downfall, Inglourious Basterds).Glorification of National Socialism or Third Reich ideology — framing Nazi ideology positively is prohibited by law.Child Sexual Abuse Material — criminal prohibition under §184b StGB.Volksverhetzung — incitement to hatred against ethnic, national, or religious groups. Applies to content targeting migrants, Jewish people, Muslim communities, or any other group.AGENT RULE — NAZI IMAGERYUsing Nazi symbols in clearly educational or critical artistic context IS legally permissible in Germany. Films like Inglourious Basterds are freely released. The distinction is: glorifying framing illegal; critical or educational framing fully legal. Agent must assess narrative intent, not just the presence of symbols.4. Indexing (Indizierung) — Practical ImpactThe Bundeszentrale für Kinder- und Jugendmedienschutz (BzKJ) can index media as harmful. Indexed films cannot be advertised publicly, sold to minors, or shown at accessible public exhibitions. Indexing grounds include: glorification of violence, incitement to hatred, glorification of drug use, deeply degrading sexual content. Indexing is rare for mainstream films but a real risk for extreme content.6.
